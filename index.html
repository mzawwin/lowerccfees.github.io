Interchange-Plus (IC+)

Transparent & Competitive

Pay What You Should, Nothing More

Interchange-Plus pricing gives you complete transparency by separating wholesale card costs from our processing markup. You see exactly what goes to the card networks and what you pay us.

  • True wholesale interchange rates passed through at cost
  • Fixed, transparent markup‚Äîno hidden fees
  • Lower costs as card mix improves
  • Ideal for businesses with consistent volume
  • Full visibility into every transaction

Best For: Businesses seeking the lowest possible rates with transparent, predictable pricing.

Dual Pricing / Cash Discounting

Eliminate Processing Fees

Zero Credit Card Processing Costs

Dual pricing (cash discounting) is a legal, compliant program that allows you to pass credit card processing costs to customers who choose to pay by card, while rewarding cash payers with a discount.

  • $0 credit card processing fees
  • Service fee offsets all credit card costs
  • Legal in all 50 states when properly implemented
  • Minimal costs for PIN debit transactions only
  • Increase profit margins instantly

Best For: Businesses looking to eliminate processing costs and maximize profitability.
